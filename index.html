sri manakula vinayagar temple

Manakula Vinayagar Temple is a Hindu temple in the Union Territory of Puducherry, India. Dedicated to the god Ganesa, it is a popular pilgrimage site featuring a traditional Dravidian Architecture that deserves high praise, the Arulmigu Manakula Vinayagar Temple is beautifully decorated with an array of friezes and stucco figurines that make the overall structure look even more impressive.

beaches

Situated in the heart of Pondicherry, this is rock-lined seashore is better suited for walking. It is adjacent to the Goubert Avenue, the main historical promenade of the city, which is lined with beautiful colonial buildings. The best way to take in the views is by walking down the entire 1.5 km-long stretch, stopping to admire the statue of Marquis Dupleix situated at one end, the Mahatma Gandhi statue in the middle, as well as the First World War Memorial. During the evenings, the beach comes alive with street food stalls, ice cream carts and balloon sellers, as well as locals taking their evening walk. medical college

Jahatma Gandhi Medical College & Research Institute (MGMCRI), founded in 2002, is a trust-run deemed college affiliated to Sri Balaji Vidyapeeth, Pondicherry. Mahatma Gandhi Medical College & Research Institute offers 250 MBBS seats along with courses under specialisation and super specialisation such as Plastic Surgery, General Medicine, Psychiatry, General Surgery, Obstetrics & Gynaecology, Paediatrics, ENT, Orthopaedics, etc. You will need to pay around 01 Cr. as total MBBS fees in MGMCRI for Mgmt. seat. The college is recognised by National Medical Council (NMC).
